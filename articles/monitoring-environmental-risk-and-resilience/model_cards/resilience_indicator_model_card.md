# Resilience Indicator Model Card

## Model purpose

This placeholder model card documents a risk and resilience indicator workflow for combining hazard, exposure, vulnerability, capacity, threshold, and recovery indicators.

## Intended use

- risk-screening support
- threshold review
- recovery trajectory monitoring
- adaptive-capacity assessment
- resilience reporting
- equity and coverage audit support

## Not intended for

- automated evacuation decisions without human review
- replacement of local emergency management judgment
- undisclosed risk ranking affecting public rights or services
- resilience claims without disaggregated indicators and uncertainty review

## Inputs

- hazard scores
- exposure scores
- vulnerability scores
- capacity scores
- redundancy and learning scores
- governance scores
- ecological condition scores
- threshold proximity
- recovery ratios

## Known limitations

- simplified scoring
- context-sensitive indicator weights
- potential aggregation bias
- missing-data sensitivity
- unequal monitoring coverage
- risk of labeling restoration as resilience without future-risk reduction
