# Equity and Recovery Notes

Risk and resilience monitoring should not reduce resilience to aggregate restoration.

## Equity questions

- Who is exposed?
- Who receives warnings?
- Who can take protective action?
- Who recovers quickly?
- Who remains displaced or harmed?
- Which communities are missing from monitoring coverage?
- Which adaptation investments reduce risk, and for whom?

## Recovery questions

- Which essential functions are restored?
- How long does restoration take?
- Does recovery reduce future risk?
- Does recovery reproduce pre-event vulnerability?
- Are ecological functions recovering, or only visible structure?
- Are marginalized populations included in recovery metrics?
