# Sustainability Indicator Model Card

## Model purpose

This placeholder model card documents a sustainability indicator integrity and feedback-review workflow.

## Intended use

- evaluate indicator integrity
- identify target gaps and threshold proximity
- detect feedback failures
- flag accountability gaps
- support strategy review
- support public evidence packages

## Not intended for

- automatic sustainability claims without governance review
- substituting indicator movement for environmental outcome evaluation
- hiding uncertainty, tradeoffs, or burden shifting
- producing public claims without data lineage and methodological transparency

## Inputs

- indicator values
- baselines
- targets
- thresholds
- metadata scores
- validation scores
- lineage scores
- uncertainty scores
- conceptual relevance scores
- material finding status
- corrective action status
- public review status

## Known limitations

- simplified scoring
- domain-specific thresholds required
- potential aggregation bias
- dependence on indicator quality
- need for ecological, justice, and governance review
