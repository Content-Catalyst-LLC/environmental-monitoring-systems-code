# Accountability and Feedback Notes

Sustainability monitoring should not stop at disclosure.

## Accountability questions

- Can indicators be traced to source data?
- Are methods, assumptions, baselines, and thresholds documented?
- Are material findings publicly reviewable?
- Can affected communities challenge evidence or interpretation?
- Does monitoring change strategy, budgets, operations, or governance?
- Are corrective actions assigned to owners with follow-up dates?

## Feedback questions

- What happens when a target is missed?
- What happens when an ecological or regulatory threshold is approached?
- What happens when an activity indicator improves but an outcome indicator does not?
- What happens when efficiency improves but absolute pressure rises?
- What happens when aggregate progress hides environmental inequality?
- What happens when monitoring reveals burden shifting?
