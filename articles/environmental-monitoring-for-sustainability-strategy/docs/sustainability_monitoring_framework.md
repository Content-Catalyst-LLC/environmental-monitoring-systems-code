# Sustainability Monitoring Framework

Environmental monitoring for sustainability strategy should connect environmental conditions to strategic review.

## Core dimensions

| Dimension | Question | Evidence artifact |
|---|---|---|
| State | What is the condition of the environmental system? | Environmental state indicators |
| Pressure | What human activities are driving environmental change? | Emissions, withdrawals, waste, land-use, throughput indicators |
| Response | What actions are being taken? | Policy, investment, restoration, circularity, adaptation records |
| Outcome | Are responses changing environmental trajectories? | Outcome indicators and trend analysis |
| Resilience | Can systems absorb disturbance, recover, adapt, or transform? | Recovery, redundancy, threshold, adaptive-capacity indicators |
| Equity | Who experiences burdens and benefits? | Disaggregated exposure and environmental justice review |
| Feedback | Do findings change strategy? | Corrective-action log |
| Accountability | Can evidence be reviewed and challenged? | Public evidence package and audit trail |
