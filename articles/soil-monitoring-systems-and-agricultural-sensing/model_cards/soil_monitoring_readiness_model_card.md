# Soil Monitoring Readiness Model Card

## Intended use

- evaluate spatial representativeness
- evaluate depth representation
- evaluate calibration quality
- evaluate telemetry completeness
- evaluate laboratory support
- evaluate remote-product agreement
- evaluate uncertainty communication
- evaluate decision readiness

## Not intended for

- replacing field agronomy
- replacing formal hydrological modeling
- treating a sensor reading as a whole-field truth
- certifying soil-health claims without lab and longitudinal evidence
