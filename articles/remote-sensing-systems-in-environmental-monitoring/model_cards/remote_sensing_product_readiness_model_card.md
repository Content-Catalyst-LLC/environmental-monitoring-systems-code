# Remote-Sensing Product Readiness Model Card

## Model purpose

This placeholder model card documents a remote-sensing product readiness scoring workflow.

## Intended use

- evaluate valid coverage
- evaluate inference quality
- identify weak proxy transparency
- flag missing field validation
- flag time-series continuity risk
- assess decision-use fit
- triage high-stakes remote-sensing products

## Not intended for

- replacing domain-scientist validation
- certifying products for public warning without governance review
- treating visual clarity as evidence strength
- treating coverage as environmental understanding
- making causal claims without supporting evidence

## Inputs

- valid coverage
- correction quality
- validation score
- uncertainty characterization
- domain suitability
- time-series continuity
- proxy transparency
- field validation
- decision fit
- high-stakes use flag

## Known limitations

- simplified scoring
- domain-specific validation required
- time-series continuity depends on product and sensor history
- proxy transparency does not guarantee interpretation quality
- decision fit depends on user role and institutional context
