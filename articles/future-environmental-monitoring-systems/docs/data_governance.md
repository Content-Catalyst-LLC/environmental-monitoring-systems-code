# Data Governance Notes

Future environmental monitoring systems should preserve the full evidence chain.

## Governance requirements

- define who decides what is monitored
- document who controls data access
- preserve metadata, provenance, uncertainty, and processing versions
- document model training data, validation domains, and known limits
- conduct coverage and equity audits
- maintain review paths for public, scientific, and institutional challenge
- ensure public outputs remain interpretable and accessible

## Sensitive considerations

Monitoring can expose vulnerable communities, sensitive ecological locations, protected species, infrastructure weaknesses, or politically contested environmental harms. Open data should be balanced with privacy, ecological protection, Indigenous data sovereignty, public interest, and misuse risk.
