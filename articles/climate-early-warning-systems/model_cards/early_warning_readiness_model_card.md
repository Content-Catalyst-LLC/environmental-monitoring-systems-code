# Early Warning Readiness Model Card

## Intended use

- evaluate forecast confidence
- evaluate risk knowledge
- evaluate protective lead time
- evaluate communication reach
- evaluate action capacity
- evaluate trust readiness
- evaluate inclusion capacity
- evaluate governance readiness
- triage high-stakes warning claims

## Not intended for

- replacing official warning authority
- certifying public safety
- hiding uncertainty or local capacity gaps
- treating alert delivery as protection
- treating coverage as effectiveness
