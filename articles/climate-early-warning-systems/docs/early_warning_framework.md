# Early Warning Evidence Framework

| Layer | Question | Evidence artifact |
|---|---|---|
| Objective | Which hazard, geography, protected population, decision use, authority, and lead-time target are being supported? | Early warning objective manifest |
| Observation | Which environmental signals, sensors, forecasts, and monitoring feeds support the hazard? | Hazard monitoring registry |
| Forecast | What is the model source, forecast horizon, confidence, uncertainty, and valid-use limit? | Forecast model card and forecast records |
| Risk knowledge | Who and what are exposed, vulnerable, and likely to be affected? | Exposure and vulnerability matrix |
| Impact threshold | What consequence level triggers warning or early action? | Impact-threshold registry |
| Warning decision | Who issued the warning, when, based on what evidence, and at what alert level? | Warning decision log |
| Communication | Which channels, languages, formats, and fallback pathways delivered the warning? | Communication-channel registry |
| Preparedness | What action was feasible, triggered, and recorded? | Preparedness and action log |
| Inclusion | Which people, places, and capacities remain weakly protected? | Coverage and inclusion audit |
| Governance | How are false alarms, missed events, trust, outcomes, and corrective actions reviewed? | Governance and after-action log |
