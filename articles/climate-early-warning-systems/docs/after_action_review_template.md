# After-Action Review Template

An early warning system should learn from events, near misses, false alarms, missed events, communication gaps, and trust failures.

## Review questions

1. Were observation feeds complete and timely?
2. Did forecast confidence and valid-use limits match the event?
3. Were exposure and vulnerability layers current?
4. Were thresholds calibrated to local impact?
5. Was warning issuance timely?
6. Was protective lead time sufficient for feasible action?
7. Did messages reach at-risk populations through usable channels?
8. Were language, disability, mobility, and digital-access needs addressed?
9. Did warning recipients understand and trust the message?
10. Which corrective actions, owners, and deadlines were assigned?
