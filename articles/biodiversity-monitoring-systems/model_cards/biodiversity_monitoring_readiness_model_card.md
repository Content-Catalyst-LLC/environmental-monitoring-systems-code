# Biodiversity Monitoring Readiness Model Card

## Model purpose

This placeholder model card documents a biodiversity-monitoring readiness and evidence-quality scoring workflow.

## Intended use

- evaluate taxonomic coverage
- evaluate spatial representativeness
- evaluate method diversity
- evaluate detectability adjustment
- evaluate observation quality
- evaluate EBV and indicator readiness
- evaluate habitat-context linkage
- evaluate uncertainty communication
- evaluate governance readiness
- triage high-stakes biodiversity claims

## Not intended for

- replacing ecological expertise
- replacing formal occupancy or abundance modeling
- treating richness as full biodiversity condition
- certifying policy claims without governance review
- reducing community or Indigenous knowledge to raw data points

## Known limitations

- simplified scoring
- detectability and abundance modeling are context-specific
- taxonomic and geographic coverage are often uneven
- EBV/indicator readiness does not equal ecological completeness
- public accountability requires evidence beyond a score
