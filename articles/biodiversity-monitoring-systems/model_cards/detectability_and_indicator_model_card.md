# Detectability and Indicator Model Card

## Purpose

This placeholder card documents future detectability, occupancy, abundance, or indicator workflows.

## Required documentation

- target taxa
- observation method
- survey effort
- season or timing
- detection probability or detection caveat
- non-detection interpretation
- indicator linkage
- EBV mapping
- habitat context
- uncertainty statement
- valid-use limits
- governance owner

## Known risks

- treating non-detection as absence
- treating habitat proxy as biological observation
- treating indicators as raw biodiversity
- underrepresenting cryptic, small, rare, seasonal, or taxonomically difficult organisms
- overrepresenting accessible sites and charismatic taxa
