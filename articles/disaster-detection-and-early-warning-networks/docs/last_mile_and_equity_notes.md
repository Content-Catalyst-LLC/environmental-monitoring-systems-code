# Last-Mile and Equity Notes

Warning equity requires more than equal message distribution.

## Key questions

- Who is exposed?
- Who receives warnings?
- Who understands warnings?
- Who trusts warnings?
- Who can take protective action?
- Who lacks transport, shelter, mobility, language access, digital access, or institutional support?
- Which warning channels fail under hazard conditions?
- Which communities are missing from preparedness exercises?
- Which groups experience delayed, partial, or failed recovery after warning failures?

## Evidence requirements

- disaggregated warning reach
- language access audit
- disability access audit
- channel redundancy assessment
- trusted messenger map
- evacuation and shelter access review
- post-event community feedback
