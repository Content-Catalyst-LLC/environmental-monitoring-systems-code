# Warning Chain Framework

Early warning networks should be evaluated as full warning chains, not as detection-only systems.

## Core layers

| Layer | Question | Evidence artifact |
|---|---|---|
| Observation | Are hazard signals detected early and reliably? | Hazard signal inventory, sensor health report |
| Integration | Are observations ingested, timestamped, quality-flagged, and interoperable? | Data schema, ingestion log |
| Assessment | Are forecasts, thresholds, and uncertainty interpreted correctly? | Forecast record, uncertainty report |
| Decision | Who decides whether to issue, update, or cancel a warning? | Authority matrix, decision log |
| Communication | Do warnings reach people through redundant and accessible channels? | Channel matrix, delivery log |
| Response | Can recipients take protective action? | Preparedness inventory, drill record |
| Equity | Are vulnerable and hard-to-reach groups included? | Last-mile reach audit |
| Learning | Does the system improve after events and exercises? | After-action review |
