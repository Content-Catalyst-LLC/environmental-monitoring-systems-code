# Warning Chain Model Card

## Model purpose

This placeholder model card documents an early warning chain readiness workflow.

## Intended use

- evaluate end-to-end warning readiness
- identify lead-time, reach, trust, response-capacity, and equity gaps
- support tabletop exercises and after-action reviews
- document warning-chain evidence for public safety review

## Not intended for

- automated evacuation orders without human authority
- replacement of emergency management judgment
- public warning without validated thresholds and communication protocols
- undisclosed scoring that affects public services or rights

## Inputs

- detection confidence
- usable lead time
- warning reach
- trust score
- response capacity
- protective action rate
- false-alarm and missed-event records
- equity gap score

## Known limitations

- simplified scoring
- context-specific hazard thresholds
- uneven availability of response data
- potential aggregation bias
- need for community review and after-action validation
