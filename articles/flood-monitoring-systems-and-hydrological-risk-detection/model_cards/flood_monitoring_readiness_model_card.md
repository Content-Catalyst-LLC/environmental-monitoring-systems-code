# Flood Monitoring Readiness Model Card

## Intended use

- evaluate gauge coverage
- evaluate rainfall coverage
- evaluate telemetry completeness
- evaluate forecast confidence
- evaluate inundation-map readiness
- evaluate protective lead time
- evaluate communication readiness
- evaluate response capacity
- evaluate governance readiness
- triage high-stakes flood-warning claims

## Not intended for

- replacing official flood warnings
- replacing hydrologist or emergency-manager judgment
- certifying evacuation decisions
- treating a score as proof of flood safety
- hiding local uncertainty, social vulnerability, or warning-access gaps
