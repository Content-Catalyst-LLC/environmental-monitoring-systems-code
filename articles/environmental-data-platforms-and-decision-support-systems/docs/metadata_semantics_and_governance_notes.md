# Metadata, Semantics, and Governance Notes

Environmental data platforms fail when data remain available but lose meaning.

## Metadata questions

- Who produced the data?
- What method produced it?
- What unit and variable definition are used?
- What spatial and temporal frames apply?
- What quality flags or uncertainty estimates exist?
- What transformations produced the displayed output?
- What access and reuse conditions apply?

## Semantic questions

- Are variables defined consistently?
- Are units compatible?
- Are temporal windows comparable?
- Are geospatial layers aligned by CRS, extent, and resolution?
- Are modeled values labeled differently from observed values?
- Are administrative records interpreted within their reporting context?

## Governance questions

- Who stewards the source?
- Who approves changes?
- Who reviews model and scenario assumptions?
- Who can modify public-facing outputs?
- How are errors corrected?
- How can affected publics review evidence?
