# Smart Water Readiness Model Card

## Intended use

- evaluate sensing coverage
- evaluate data-quality validity
- evaluate telemetry completeness
- evaluate latency readiness
- evaluate anomaly confidence
- evaluate response capacity
- evaluate model readiness
- evaluate control safety
- evaluate governance readiness

## Not intended for

- replacing operator judgment
- certifying autonomous control
- replacing cybersecurity review
- treating dashboards as evidence of response capacity
