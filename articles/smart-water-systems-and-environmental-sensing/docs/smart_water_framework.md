# Smart Water Evidence and Response Framework

| Layer | Question | Evidence artifact |
|---|---|---|
| Objective | What service goal, domain, asset, variable, and response expectation is being supported? | Smart water objective manifest |
| Sensing | Which sensors, meters, and instruments observe the system? | Sensor and asset registry |
| Telemetry | How complete, timely, and reliable are communications? | Telemetry status records |
| Validation | How are drift, fouling, false values, and stale data flagged? | QA/QC validation policy |
| Context | How are signals linked to assets, pressure zones, treatment stages, sewersheds, and watersheds? | Asset and domain registry |
| Analytics | What thresholds, anomaly rules, models, or digital twins interpret signals? | Threshold registry and model cards |
| Response | Who investigates, confirms, samples, dispatches, adjusts, or communicates? | Response and work-order log |
| Control | What semi-automated or automated actions are allowed? | Control and fail-safe policy |
| Governance | How are privacy, cybersecurity, capacity, equity, public caveats, and revision handled? | Governance log |
