# Public Evidence Package Template

Required fields:

- water domain
- monitored assets and geography
- monitoring period
- measured variables
- sensor and meter methods
- telemetry and latency caveats
- QA/QC and validation approach
- alert and threshold rationale
- response workflow
- model or digital twin caveats
- control and fail-safe policy
- privacy and cybersecurity notes
- equity and capacity caveats
- governance owner
