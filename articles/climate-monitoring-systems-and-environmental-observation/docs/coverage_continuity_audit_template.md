# Coverage and Continuity Audit Template

A climate monitoring system should periodically review whether records remain adequate for the climate claims being made.

## Review questions

1. Which climate variable and decision use does this record support?
2. Which platforms, instruments, stations, or missions produced the record?
3. Are station history, platform history, algorithm history, and processing lineage complete?
4. Are calibration, cross-calibration, and overlap periods documented?
5. What time steps are missing or invalid?
6. Which spatial domains are under-observed?
7. Which ocean, polar, cryosphere, mountain, island, or lower-capacity regions are weakly represented?
8. Which platform transitions may affect trends or anomalies?
9. Which homogenization decisions changed the record?
10. Which public claims exceed the record's evidence strength?
