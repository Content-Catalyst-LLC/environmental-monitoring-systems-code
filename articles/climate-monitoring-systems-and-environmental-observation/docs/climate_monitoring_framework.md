# Climate Monitoring Evidence Framework

| Layer | Question | Evidence artifact |
|---|---|---|
| Objective | Which climate variable, domain, geography, baseline, temporal scale, and decision use are being supported? | Climate monitoring objective manifest |
| Platform | Which stations, satellites, ocean platforms, cryosphere sites, or specialty networks produce the evidence? | Climate observation platform registry |
| Metadata | Can future users reconstruct instrument, method, site, platform, algorithm, and processing context? | Metadata and provenance registry |
| Calibration | Are reference standards, sensor transitions, overlap periods, and intercomparisons documented? | Calibration and intercomparison log |
| Observation | What was observed, where, when, by which platform, with what unit and QC status? | Climate observation records |
| Homogenization | Were non-climatic break points, station moves, method changes, or platform shifts identified? | Homogenization and adjustment log |
| Record | What versioned climate data record was constructed and with what valid-use caveats? | Climate data record manifest |
| Indicator | Which baseline, anomaly, trend, or climate indicator was produced? | Indicator and anomaly table |
| Coverage | Which spatial, temporal, ocean, polar, vertical, or lower-capacity gaps remain? | Coverage and continuity audit |
| Governance | Who stewards, revises, cites, archives, and publicly caveats the record? | Climate monitoring governance log |
