# Reanalysis Linkage Model Card

## Intended use

- document climate records used alongside reanalysis
- distinguish observation from model-assisted reconstruction
- track product version and access path
- expose caveats and uncertainty
- connect reanalysis outputs to climate monitoring records

## Core caveat

Reanalysis products extend the interpretive value of observations, but they are not direct observations. Any public claim should distinguish direct measurement, gridded climate record, and model-assisted reconstruction.
