# Climate Record Quality Model Card

## Intended use

- evaluate record continuity
- evaluate metadata completeness
- evaluate calibration quality
- evaluate homogenization readiness
- evaluate spatial coverage
- evaluate version control
- evaluate uncertainty communication
- evaluate governance readiness
- triage high-stakes climate claims

## Not intended for

- replacing expert climate assessment
- replacing formal attribution studies
- certifying climate records as official
- hiding uncertainty or observational asymmetry
- treating raw observations as climate records
