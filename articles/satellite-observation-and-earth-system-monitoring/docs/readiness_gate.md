# Deployment Readiness Gate

Before a satellite product is used for forecasting, climate reporting, public communication, hazard response, policy, infrastructure planning, or accountability, it should pass a readiness gate.

## Readiness checks

1. Mission, orbit, sensor, resolution, and revisit match the environmental question.
2. Product metadata include mission, sensor, sensing mode, product level, variable, unit, grid, cadence, and version.
3. Calibration and geolocation records are present.
4. Retrieval method is documented.
5. Validation evidence exists for relevant geography, season, surface type, and environmental regime.
6. Uncertainty, confidence, caveats, and valid-use limits are visible.
7. Continuity across missions and algorithm versions is documented where trend interpretation matters.
8. In situ and model integration are documented where claims require more than satellite evidence alone.
9. Decision-use and user roles are explicit.
10. Product governance and public caveat policy are defined.
