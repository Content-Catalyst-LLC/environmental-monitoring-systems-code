# Proxy, Uncertainty, and Continuity Notes

Satellite products can be visually powerful while remaining inferentially complex.

## Proxy questions

- Is the product measured, retrieved, classified, modeled, forecast, or scenario-based?
- What physical signal is being measured?
- What environmental state is inferred from that signal?
- What assumptions connect the signal to the claim?
- Where is the retrieval valid?
- Where is the product weak?

## Uncertainty questions

- Is uncertainty provided as a layer, score, interval, quality flag, caveat, or validation statistic?
- Is uncertainty visible to downstream users?
- Are modeled, interpolated, and classified outputs labeled distinctly?
- Is product confidence known across regions, seasons, surface types, and atmospheric conditions?

## Continuity questions

- Are mission transitions documented?
- Are sensor overlaps available?
- Is cross-calibration performed?
- Are algorithm and product versions tracked?
- Is reprocessing needed for long-term trend analysis?
