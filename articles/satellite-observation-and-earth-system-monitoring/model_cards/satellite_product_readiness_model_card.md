# Satellite Product Readiness Model Card

## Model purpose

This placeholder model card documents a satellite product readiness scoring workflow.

## Intended use

- evaluate usable coverage
- evaluate retrieval quality
- identify continuity gaps
- flag weak proxy transparency
- flag missing validation
- assess decision-use fit
- triage high-stakes satellite products

## Not intended for

- replacing domain-scientist validation
- certifying products for public warning without governance review
- treating coverage as evidence strength by itself
- hiding uncertainty behind composite scores
- making policy claims without product caveats

## Inputs

- usable coverage
- calibration score
- validation score
- uncertainty characterization
- domain suitability
- continuity score
- proxy transparency
- in situ validation
- decision fit
- high-stakes use flag

## Known limitations

- simplified scoring
- domain-specific validation required
- continuity depends on mission and algorithm history
- proxy transparency does not guarantee interpretation quality
- decision fit depends on user role and institutional context
