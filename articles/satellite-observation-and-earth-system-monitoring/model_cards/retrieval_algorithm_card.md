# Retrieval Algorithm Card

## Purpose

This placeholder card documents how an orbital signal becomes a satellite product.

## Product family

Satellite observation and Earth-system monitoring products.

## Evidence status

A retrieved satellite product should not be described as direct environmental observation unless the product explicitly represents measured signal. Most products transform radiance, reflectance, brightness temperature, backscatter, altimetry, lidar waveform, or related signals into geophysical variables, classifications, or indicators.

## Required documentation

- source mission
- instrument
- sensing mode
- spectral or measurement characteristics
- retrieval algorithm
- input data
- calibration dependency
- validation datasets
- uncertainty method
- known failure regimes
- valid-use statement
- product version
- decision-use limits

## Known risks

- proxy forgetting
- orbital overconfidence
- weak in situ validation
- algorithm version drift
- sensor-transition discontinuity
- cloud or atmosphere bias
- resolution mismatch
- public overinterpretation
