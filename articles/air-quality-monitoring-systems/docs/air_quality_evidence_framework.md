# Air Quality Evidence Framework

| Layer | Question | Evidence artifact |
|---|---|---|
| Objective | Which pollutants, geography, populations, time scales, and decisions are being supported? | Air quality monitoring objective manifest |
| Network design | Are monitor locations, platform types, and coverage choices justified? | Siting rationale and network map |
| Sensor registry | Which instruments measure which pollutants under what valid-use limits? | Air quality sensor registry |
| Observation | What pollutant concentration was measured, where, when, by which platform, and with what QC status? | Pollutant observation records |
| Calibration | Are reference comparison, collocation, correction, drift, and maintenance documented? | Calibration and collocation log |
| Quality control | Were missingness, drift, humidity, outliers, cross-sensitivity, and sensor faults flagged? | QC policy and QC flag table |
| Meteorological context | Are pollutant patterns interpreted with wind, humidity, temperature, stability, and dispersion context? | Meteorological context table |
| Exposure and equity | Are measurements linked to population, vulnerability, source proximity, and environmental justice concerns? | Exposure and equity layer |
| Communication | Are dashboards, AQI, alerts, and reports clear, caveated, and public-health relevant? | Dashboard and alert specification |
| Governance | Do findings lead to advisories, enforcement, intervention, public access, or corrective action? | Governance and intervention log |
