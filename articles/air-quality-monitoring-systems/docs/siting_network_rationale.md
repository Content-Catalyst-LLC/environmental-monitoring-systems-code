# Siting and Network Rationale

Air quality monitoring depends on where monitors are placed and what atmospheric and social questions they are designed to answer.

## Required siting fields

- monitoring purpose
- pollutant focus
- platform type
- source proximity
- expected exposure pathway
- population relevance
- meteorological representativeness
- height and microenvironment
- nearby roads, industry, ports, warehouses, schools, hospitals, or housing
- spatial scale represented
- valid-use caveat
- environmental justice relevance
