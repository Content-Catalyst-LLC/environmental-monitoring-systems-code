# QA/QC Review Template

A water quality monitoring system should review both measurement validity and evidence-use validity.

## Review questions

1. Were sensors calibrated before and after deployment?
2. Were fouling, drift, and field conditions documented?
3. Were blanks, duplicates, or control samples used where appropriate?
4. Were samples preserved and transported within valid holding times?
5. Were method detection limits and reporting limits recorded?
6. Were non-detects handled transparently?
7. Were observations paired with flow, stage, event, or sampling-condition context?
8. Were proxy relationships validated with direct or laboratory measurements?
9. Were threshold sources and valid-use rules documented?
10. Were public claims caveated according to method and representativeness limits?
